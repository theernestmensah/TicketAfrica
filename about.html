We are building the definitive infrastructure for the African event economy, enabling seamless transactions across borders, currencies, and cultures.

Our Mission

Ticket Africa was founded to solve the fragmentation in the continent's ticketing industry. Before us, organizing a pan-African tour or a localized festival meant dealing with multiple payment gateways, unreliable QR systems, and a lack of local context like USSD or Mobile Money.

Today, we provide a unified, world-class platform that gives event organizers everywhere access to enterprise-grade tools, while ensuring attendees enjoy a premium, frictionless purchase experience regardless of their location or payment method.
