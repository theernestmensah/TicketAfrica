Zero Tolerance for Fraud

We protect buyers and organizers by ensuring every ticket is 100% authentic. Learn how our technology prevents counterfeits and unauthorized resale.

Encrypted Dynamic QR Codes

Every ticket generated by Ticket Africa contains a dynamic, encrypted QR code. Screenshots and photocopies will not scan at the door. Our proprietary scanner app constantly verifies codes against live server-side data, making duplication physically impossible.

Secure Title Transfer

When you buy a ticket on Ticket Africa, the digital asset is bound to your account identity. If you wish to gift a ticket, you must explicitly transfer it via the app, generating a brand new hash for the receiver. The original ticket is instantly invalidated.

Scalping Prevention

We actively monitor buying patterns to defeat bots and mass-scalpers. We enforce strict ticket limits per user account during massive drops, and our internal rules engine blocks suspicious IP ranges and high-velocity purchasing tactics automatically.

Official Resale Marketplace

We provide the only authorized secondary market for sold-out events. Tickets resold outside our platform (e.g., social media or scalper websites) are strictly prohibited and will be voided at the point of entry. Our resale caps prevent price gouging.
