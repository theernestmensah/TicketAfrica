About This Event

The most anticipated night of the year returns. Afrobeats Night: Grand Finale brings together Ghana's biggest musical talent for one unforgettable evening at Accra Sports Stadium. Headlining the night is international superstar Stonebwoy, supported by an explosive lineup of Afrobeats, Highlife, and Dancehall acts.

This is not just a concert — it is a cultural celebration. Expect state-of-the-art production, pyrotechnics, and a crowd energy that will define 2026. Gates open at 6:30 PM. Main act begins at 10:00 PM. Supporting acts from 8:00 PM.

Lineup includes:

  • Sefa
