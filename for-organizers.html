Platform Features

Everything you need to run events at scale

Create Events in Minutes
Upload artwork, set ticket tiers, configure pricing and inventory. Go live in under 5 minutes. No technical knowledge required.
Multiple Ticket Tiers
Create unlimited ticket types — General Admission, VIP, Tables, Early Bird, Presale. Set custom inventory and per-order limits.
Promo Codes & Discounts
Generate unlimited promo codes. Set percentage or fixed discounts, usage limits, and expiry dates. Track redemption in real-time.
Real-Time Analytics
Track ticket sales, revenue, conversion rates, and attendee demographics as they happen. Export reports as CSV or PDF.
Fast Payouts
Receive your revenue within 2 business days of the event. Pay out to mobile money, bank account, or any supported method.
Fraud-Protected QR Tickets
Every ticket carries a unique, dynamic QR code. Our scanner app validates authenticity in real-time, with offline mode for venues with poor connectivity.
Attendee Management
View full attendee lists. Export to CSV. Send event update messages via SMS or email directly from your dashboard.
Reserved Seating Maps
Configure venue seat maps for assigned seating events. Attendees choose their exact seat at checkout. Supported for theatres, stadiums, and conference halls.
USSD Ticket Sales
Reach buyers without smartphones. USSD ticket purchasing via *123*88# expands your audience to feature phone users across all 5 markets.
