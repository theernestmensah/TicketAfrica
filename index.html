Fraud Protection

Every ticket is authentic.
Every scan is verified.

Ticket fraud is a major problem in African markets. We've built multi-layer protection to ensure that every QR code issued is genuine and every entry is legitimate.

Dynamic QR Codes
Each ticket generates a unique, time-sensitive QR code that cannot be photocopied or duplicated. Invalidated immediately on transfer.
Real-Time Validation
Scanner app connects to our servers in real-time. Duplicate scan attempts are instantly flagged and denied — even with offline backup mode.
Device Fingerprinting
Bot detection and per-device purchase limits prevent scalpers from bulk-buying and reselling at exploitative prices.

Verify a Ticket

Enter a ticket code or scan a QR to instantly verify its authenticity before the event.
