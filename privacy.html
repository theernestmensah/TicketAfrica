Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

At Ticket Africa, we collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, purchase a ticket, or contact us. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Payment transaction data (processed securely by our payment partners).
  • Information about the events you attend.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including:

  • Processing ticket purchases and sending event reminders.
  • Verifying identity and preventing fraud.
  • Sending administrative messages and security alerts.
  • Providing customer support.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our services (e.g., with event organizers for check-in purposes) or as required by law.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can update your account information at any time. You may also opt-out of receiving promotional emails from us by following the instructions in those emails.
