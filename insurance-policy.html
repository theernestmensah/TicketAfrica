Event Insurance Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

1. Overview

Ticket Africa offers optional event insurance to both organizers and ticket buyers. This policy outlines the coverage, exclusions, and claims process for our insurance products.

2. For Ticket Buyers (Ticket Protection)

Ticket Protection is an optional add-on available during checkout. It covers the ticket price and service fees if you are unable to attend the event due to unforeseen circumstances, including:

  • Illness or injury (with medical certificate).
  • Travel delays (verifiable transportation failure).
  • Death of an immediate family member.
  • Jury service or military deployment.

3. For Organizers (Event Cancellation Insurance)

Organizers can purchase comprehensive coverage for event cancellation, postponement, or abandonment due to reasons beyond their control, such as:

  • Extreme weather conditions (Force Majeure).
  • Venue damage (fire, flood).
  • Key artist non-appearance (optional rider).
  • National mourning or public health mandates.

4. Exclusions

Coverage does not apply in cases of:

  • Change of mind by the ticket holder.
  • Pre-existing medical conditions not disclosed.
  • Fraudulent claims.
  • War, terrorism, or civil unrest (unless explicitly covered by a rider).

5. Claims Process

To file a claim, please visit our Help Center or email claims@ticketafrica.com within 7 days of the event. You will be required to provide proof of the covered incident.
