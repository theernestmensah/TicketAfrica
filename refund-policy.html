Refund & Insurance

Refund Policy

Ticket Africa operates on a conditional refund policy. Generally, all ticket sales are final and non-refundable unless:

  • The event is cancelled and not rescheduled.
  • The event is postponed and the new date is not suitable for you.
  • There was a technical error during the booking process resulting in double payment.

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the scheduled event date or the announcement of cancellation/postponement.

Ticket Insurance Policy

Ticket Africa offers an optional Ticket Insurance for selected high-value events. This insurance provides coverage for:

  • Personal illness or injury preventing attendance (medical certificate required).
  • Death of an immediate family member.
  • Unforeseen severe weather conditions preventing travel.
  • Loss of employment or involuntary relocation.

Insurance premiums are non-refundable and must be purchased at the time of ticket booking. Coverage is provided by our third-party insurance partners.
